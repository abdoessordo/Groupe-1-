About

The A&M Mechatronics is a non-profit club created in 2014 by engineering students sharing the same passion for mechatronics, contributes since its foundation to ensure a panel of activities of high level technical characteristics. The club aims to develop knowledge through the formats of face-to-face and remote workshops, the realization of innovative technological projects to participate in national and international competitions. the club also organizes visits, meetings, conferences and webinars led by professionals and this in order to prepare our future engineers to integrate into a professional group.
