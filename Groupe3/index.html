ECA, your path to a bright professional career.

The promotion of vocational training is an urgent necessity to put Morocco in the capacity to meet the challenges of economic competitiveness ”- His Majesty the King Mohamed VI, speech to the Nation on the occasion of the 66th anniversary of the king's revolution and the people. It is from this majestic directive that ENSAM Casablanca inaugurated its academy in order to train its students mainly to acquire new professional skills and thus meet the country in a position to meet the challenges challenges of economic competitiveness. In order to make the CVs of its students more relevant thus meeting the current needs of national and multinational companies, the ECA increasingly seeks to gain the trust of recognized academies facilitating the access of graduates to the business world. For the year 2020/2021, the ECA has partnered with the nine giants: PaloAlto, Juniper, Cisco, Huawei, Oracle, Fortinet, Comptia, Amazon and Redhat.
