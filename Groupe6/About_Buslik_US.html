About Us

BusLik Mobilités imagines, organizes and finances transport for all Morocco City.

Every day in Morocco 1 million trips are made by Moroccans using one of the largest public transport networks in the world. Whether for business trips or for their studies, equipped with a Buslik pass (card or phone) or an occasional ticket, travelers can take the 150 bus lines.

Our missions

To operate all of these lines on a daily basis, Buslik Mobilités contracts with transport companies such as Alsa, CityBus and many others, which are responsible for the proper functioning of the lines entrusted to them and for reaching the levels. of the quality of service that is set for them (punctuality, accessibility, information, safety, cleanliness).
The needs of Moroccan People in terms of transport and mobility are constantly evolving. It is the local elected representatives meeting in the Mobilities board of directors who take all the major decisions allowing to anticipate, adapt and modernize the transport offer to the needs of travelers.
