Contactless payment methods at your disposal

Many contactless payment services are available for both regular and occasional travelers, to buy your tickets in advance or at the last second if you have no change. In addition to the already existing BusLik packages, two new passes are now available, as well as the purchase of transport tickets on smartphones to no longer have to go through the machines, and finally payment by text message for those who have no change.

Ticket-SMS for buses

Fisrt In Morocco , the purchase of a bus ticket in the inner suburbs can be done using your phone and without the need for change: by sending a code, you will receive a ticket in the form of an SMS and will be debited directly on your mobile bill.

BusLik Ticket

Buslik Easy is a contactless pass to load your tickets and certain short packages. Accessible to all and without obligation, you can load your ticket books, tickets for a single day, weeks, months, various packages

Buslik with your smartphone

The new services, available on the Buslik application, allow you to buy and / or top up your passes:

  • Load your Buslik pass. You must have an NFC phone with at least Android version 6.0, then install the Buslik application. Then go to the Purchase tab and let yourself be guided. So, you no longer need to go to a terminal or a ticket office / station and stand in line!
  • Load titles / packages on your smartphone and validate them with it. This second service is available either on Android smartphones fitted with a SIM card with NFC function, via the Buslik application, or on certain Samsung smartphones incorporating a secure chip which allows the storage of tickets. This service is added to cardboard tickets and the Buslik pass by offering a service better suited to new uses.
